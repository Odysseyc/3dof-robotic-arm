\# Robot Kinematics



\## Forward Kinematics



Forward kinematics maps joint angles to the end-effector pose.



For this project, the initial robot model is a 3-DOF arm with:



\- Base rotation

\- Shoulder pitch

\- Elbow pitch



The goal is to later extend this model into:

\- inverse kinematics

\- trajectory planning

\- simulation

\- physical control
